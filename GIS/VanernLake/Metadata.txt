Forest and wetland spatial data from Copernicus: 
https://cws-download.eea.europa.eu/pan-european/hrl/HRL_Summary_for_publication_v11.pdf
http://land.copernicus.eu/pan-european/high-resolution-layers/wetlands/view
http://land.copernicus.eu/pan-european/high-resolution-layers/forests/forest-type/view

Both at 20 m resolution, from satellite imagery

Forest: coniferous and deciduous reclassified as 1, everything else as no data
Wetland: used all types classified as wetland in original layer and reclassified as 1, everything else as no data

Projected coordinate system: grs80_Lambert_Azimuthal_Equal_Area
